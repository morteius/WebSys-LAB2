Day 1: Friday Market Day

February 20, 2026 Elaine Paras 5 min read adventures, personal

We were tasked to write a blog post about our life. Sakto at may lakad ako ngayong friday, so I thought it would be perfect to write about my day.

Market Day

Sa municipality namin which is Camalig, every Tuesday and Friday is market day. It's always a lively scene with vendors selling fresh produce, street food, and all sorts of goods. Pero para maiba naman, I decided to go to the market with CJ. Sa Daraga pala dapat mas maayos mamalengke, kaso nasa Albay na kami kaya doon na lang.

After bumili ng gulay, we decided na mag-ice cream muna. It was a sunny day, kaya perfect ang ice cream. Then, tumambay kami sa park for a bit, just talking and enjoying the weather.

Thank you CJ sa ayskrim!

Going Home

Uwian time na kaya nag-hintay muna kami ng jeep. Dapat ay susunduin ko pa kapatid ko sa school, pero since alanganin na ang time, I messaged my sister na siya na lang muna ang mag-sundo. Sobrang traffic, kaya naka-uwi ako ng mga almost 6pm na. But it was a fun day, and I'm glad I got to spend it with CJ.

Nag-lakad lang ako pauwi after ng jeepney ride so I get to enjoy the view kahit grabe ang polusyon sa paligid. I arrived at home at nag-pahinga for awhile then ate the food CJ made and shared it with our bunso.

Make the best out of every day, even if it's just a simple market day. It's the little moments that make life special.

I had a great day exploring the market, enjoying some ice cream, and spending time with CJ. It was a nice break from the usual routine and a reminder to appreciate the simple moments in life.

— Elaine

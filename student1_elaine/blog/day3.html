Day 3: Monday Blues

February 23, 2026 Elaine Paras 5 min read monday, blues, rest

I woke up feeling a bit sluggish today. Monday mornings are always a challenge for me – the weekend feels like it's slipping away, and I'm not quite ready to dive back into the routine.

Wake Up Call

My mom called me to remind me that I had class today. I was still half-asleep and groggy, but her voice helped me get out of bed and start my day. Sometimes a gentle reminder is all you need to get going.

Getting Ready for the Day

After doing my morning routine, I felt a bit more energized. I took a moment to look at my schedule for the day and made sure I had everything I needed – my binder, pen, and a small snack to keep me going. I reviewed my notes for the day ahead and made a quick list of tasks to tackle.

A message popped up on my phone – it was from our professor. I quickly read the message and continued studying but with another subject. Our quiz for her that afternoon is to be rescheduled to the next meeting, so I have a bit more time to prepare. I'm trying to stay positive and not let the change in plans throw me off too much.

Change is hard, but it's also an opportunity to grow and adapt.

A good luck wish to myself for the day ahead!

— Elaine

Day 3: Casual Sunday

February 22, 2026 Elaine Paras 4 min read personal, rest

Sunday was a quiet and relaxing day. Sometimes you just need those days to recharge and reset.

Sleeping In

I woke up late today – no alarms, no rushing. Just the sunlight creeping through my window and the sound of my mama's voice calling me for breakfast.

Simple Breakfast

Had a simple breakfast while scrolling through my phone. Checked some messages and saw that CJ was also having a chill day at home. We briefly talked about our plans for the upcoming week.

Review Mode

Spent the afternoon reviewing for my upcoming quizzes. Opened my notes for Web Systems, Networking 1, and IM 1 then tried to focus, pero wala talagang pumapasok sa utak ko huhu. Pero at least I got to review a bit and feel a little more prepared para bukas.

I listened to random music while reviewing. It was nice to have that background noise while I tried to focus. Hanggang sa nakatulog na lang ako.

I also made a to-do list for the week. Feels productive even though today was mostly rest.

Rest is not idle, it's preparation.

Here's to a productive week ahead! Ready to tackle whatever comes.

— Elaine

Day 4: Boyfriend Daw Moments

February 23, 2026 · Monday CJ Mape 7 min read coding, calls, food

Woke up early around 7. My stomach was still aching from the past few days, but I was really hungry since I lost most of my appetite when this whole stomach thing began. Made egg garlic fried rice to start the day right.

Breakfast of Champions

The hunger finally won over the stomach ache. I craved something solid and comforting, so I whipped up some egg garlic fried rice. Simple but hits different when you haven't eaten properly in days. Actually finished the whole plate — a small victory considering how weak my appetite has been. Hopefully this means my stomach is finally recovering.

Studying for Ma'am Jevah's Quiz

After breakfast, I opened my notes and started reviewing for the upcoming quiz in Ma'am Jevah's class. Tried to focus despite my stomach still being iffy. Went through the modules, highlighted key points, and did some practice questions. Was mentally preparing for the worst.

Surprise! Quiz Cancelled

Then the news came — our online quiz was cancelled. Surprise, surprise! Can't say I was upset about it. All that morning review, and now I have extra time to prepare. Sometimes the universe just throws you a bone. Guess my stomach and I get a little break today.

Blog Coding Session with Elaine

With the quiz cancelled, I shifted focus to our duo project. I called Elaine and we started working on this blog together. We were talking thru call while coding, having small talks from time to time. I played some music in the background to set the vibe. It's nice having someone to code with — makes the whole process less lonely and more fun.

We'd be deep in code, then one of us would randomly say something and we'd end up laughing for a few minutes before getting back to work. Productive and entertaining at the same time.

The Boyfriend Moment

At one point during our call, I overheard Elaine's mom in the background. Turns out she thought I was Elaine's boyfriend calling. I quickly laughed. Elaine just giggled at her mother, but the moment definitely added some entertainment to our coding session. Sorry Elaine, but your mom made my day with that one.

Afternoon Nap

After a productive coding session, I felt tired (still recovering, I guess). So I took a nap. Slept for a couple of hours — much needed rest after days of stomach issues and late-night studying. Woke up feeling a bit more refreshed.

Evening Study & Networking Activities

Woke up in the evening and got back to work. Started studying again and worked on my activities for our Networking subject. Readings, assignments, the usual. Tried to knock out as much as I could while I had the energy.

Back to Bed

After finishing my networking tasks, I called it a night. My stomach still isn't 100%, but it's getting better. Progress. Headed to bed hoping tomorrow will be even better.

Cancelled quiz, coding calls, and accidentally being mistaken as a boyfriend. Productive day with plot twists.

Hoping my stomach is fully back tomorrow. Goodnight.

— CJ

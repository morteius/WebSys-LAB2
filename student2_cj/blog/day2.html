Day 2: Workout & Upset Stomach

February 24, 2026 CJ Mape 3 min read

Today was a mixed day – got a good workout in but ended up with an upset stomach.

Bodyweight Workout

Started the day with a bodyweight workout here in the boarding house. Just push-ups, squats, lunges, and some core exercises. Nothing too intense but enough to get the blood flowing. Gotta stay active even on busy days.

It's not much compared to the gym, but it works when you can't go out. Plus, saving money while staying fit – win-win.

Worked on the Project

Spent a good chunk of the afternoon coding. We're making progress on the portfolio pages. Got the navigation working properly and started on the about page. It's satisfying seeing things come together.

Upset Stomach

Unfortunately, later in the evening, my stomach started acting up. Not sure if it was something I ate or just one of those days. Had to take it easy and rest for a bit. Definitely not fun, but it is what it is.

Hoping to feel better tomorrow so I can get back to coding and maybe play some more Fable.

Some days are just like that – take the win where you can.

Rest and recover.
CJ

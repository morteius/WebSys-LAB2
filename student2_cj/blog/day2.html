Day 2: Workout & Upset Stomach

February 24, 2026 CJ Mape 4 min read personal, fitness

Today was a mixed day – got a good workout in but ended up with an upset stomach. Still managed to get some coding done though.

Morning Bodyweight Workout

Started the day with a bodyweight workout here in the boarding house. No gym access today, but that's not an excuse to skip. Did push-ups, squats, lunges, and some core exercises. Nothing too intense but enough to get the blood flowing.

Did 3 sets of 15 push-ups, 20 squats, 15 lunges each leg, and held planks for 45 seconds. Feeling the burn but in a good way. It's not much compared to the gym, but it works when you can't go out. Plus, saving money while staying fit – win-win.

Worked on the Project

Spent a good chunk of the afternoon coding. We're making progress on the portfolio pages. Got the navigation working properly and started on the about page. It's satisfying seeing things come together.

Elaine sent me some design ideas for the blog layout. We're trying to keep things consistent across both our pages. The baby blue theme is really starting to look nice.

Upset Stomach

Unfortunately, later in the evening, my stomach started acting up. Not sure if it was something I ate or just one of those days. Had to take it easy and rest for a bit. Definitely not fun, but it is what it is.

Drank some warm water and tried to rest. Couldn't do much else. Hoping to feel better tomorrow so I can get back to coding and maybe play some more Fable.

Some days are just like that – take the win where you can, rest when you need to.

Even with the upset stomach, I'm glad I got the workout in and made progress on the project. Small wins add up.

— CJ

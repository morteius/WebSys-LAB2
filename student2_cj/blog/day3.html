Day 3: Art Pop Market & Fable Night

February 25, 2026 CJ Mape 4 min read

Another day at the Art Pop Market with Elaine, then more Fable when I got home.

Back to the Market

Elaine and I went back to the Legazpi Art Pop Market today. It's becoming our little thing, I guess. We took our time exploring all the booths – didn't rush like last time.

Vintage Game Art

I found that booth again with the vintage video game art prints. This time I actually bought a small piece – it's a pixel art of an old RPG scene. Gonna put it up in my room. Elaine got herself a keychain made from recycled materials. Supporting local artists feels good.

We also took another set of photobooth pictures. This time we did funny faces. The photobooth strips are starting to pile up – in a good way.

Street Food

Grabbed some fish balls and kwek-kwek before heading home. Hit the spot. Hopefully no upset stomach this time!

Fable: The Lost Chapters

When I got home, I jumped back into Fable. Made some progress in the main story and did a few more side quests. I'm trying to be a good character in this playthrough – we'll see how long that lasts.

There's just something about this game. The humor, the choices, the world. It's comfort gaming for sure.

Good day with friends, good night with games. No complaints.

Another day, another adventure.
CJ

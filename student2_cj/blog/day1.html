Day 1: Getting Started & Fable

February 23, 2026 CJ Mape 4 min read

Today we started our duo project, and after hanging out with Elaine, I went home and played Fable: The Lost Chapters.

Working on the Project

Elaine and I finally started working on our portfolio blog today. We planned out the structure and decided on the baby blue theme. It's nice finally putting ideas into code. We worked together for a few hours, figuring out the layout and what pages we need.

Art Pop Market

After working, we decided to check out the Legazpi Art Pop Market again. Saw some cool vintage video game art prints – almost bought one but decided to save my money for now. Maybe next time!

Night Session: Fable: The Lost Chapters

When I got home, I booted up Fable: The Lost Chapters. It's an older game but honestly, it still holds up. The music, the atmosphere, the choices – takes me back to simpler times. I played for a couple of hours, just exploring Albion and doing side quests.

There's something relaxing about playing these classic games after a productive day. It's my way of unwinding.

Productive day, classic game night. Balance.

Looking forward to more progress tomorrow.
CJ
